ANNETTE JOSEPH STYLE
@annettejosephstyle @stylephotoworkshops

Lifestyle Expert, Author of Cocktail Italiano and Named One of The Salonniere's 100 Best Party Hosts
Annette Joseph is a nationally known expert on entertaining, cooking, photo styling, and interior design and is a stylist for numerous lifestyle magazines. With a solid background in everything from DIY projects to food, Annette has worked on countless projects with cookbook authors and top-notch magazines such as Better Homes & Gardens, Redbook, and People, and is a lifestyle contributor and entertaining expert on the Today Show, Better Mornings, and Martha Stewart Sirius Radio. Her recipes and party ideas have been featured in the Huffington Post, Southern Living, and Woman's World, among others. Annette splits her time between Atlanta and Tuscany, Italy, where she and her husband are restoring a medieval fortress.

Mandy Kellogg Rye

Waiting On Martha
@waitingonmartha

Retailer, Blogger and Founder of Waiting On Martha
Waiting On Martha boasts a lifestyle blog, e-commerce channel, and brick and mortar home and gift shop in Atlanta. Born under the premise of inspiring others to create a beautiful everyday, Mandy’s take on joyful living, from recipes and entertaining to home decorating and travel, resonates deeply with her readers and attracts brand partnerships with the likes of Williams Sonoma, Pottery Barn, Frontgate, and Whole Foods just to name a few. Mandy’s beloved brand was originally an homage to her favorite personality, Martha Stewart, but over the years, she has made quite the name for herself being featured in top magazines such as House Beautiful, Harper’s Bazaar, Lonny and many more. When Mandy isn’t Martha, you can find her at home with her husband, Anthony, and King Charles Cavalier, Addison.
